## Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1)

### Day 1: Critical First Steps

#### 1. Apply for Florida Resale Certificate
**Why:** This unlocks wholesale purchasing tax-free  
**Where:** Florida Department of Revenue  
**URL:** https://floridarevenue.com/taxes/register  
**Cost:** Free  
**Time:** 30 minutes  
**Documents needed:**
- LLC name and registration number
- EIN
- Business address (food trailer location)
- Estimated monthly sales (estimate $1,000–2,000)

**What you get:** Certificate number to use with wholesalers

#### 2. Set Up eBay Seller Account
**Steps:**
1. Go to ebay.com/seller
2. Link your business bank account
3. Add credit/debit card for fees
4. Subscribe to "Starter Store" ($4.95/month with annual plan)

**Why Starter:**
- 250 free listings/month
- Lower final value fees (12.35% vs 12.9%)
- Can upgrade anytime as you grow

#### 3. List First 10 Winco Lots (Test Batch)

**Your Inventory:**

### Week 4: Test Amazon FBM

#### List 2–3 Winco Items on Amazon (FBM)

**Steps:**
1. Seller Central → Inventory → Add a Product
2. Search for your item (may not exist, create new listing)
3. Select "I will ship this item myself" (FBM)
4. Set price 15% above eBay to cover higher fees
5. Set handling time: 1 day
6. Shipping template: Standard shipping

**Important:**
- No FBA = no Prime badge = slower sales
- Higher fees than eBay
- Stricter performance metrics
- Test only, don't commit inventory yet

#### Continue eBay Scaling
- List new wholesale inventory as it arrives
- Aim for 100+ active listings
- Target: $500+ in eBay sales this month

## Phase 3: Amazon Ungating (Months 2–3)

### Month 2: Submit Amazon Ungating Application

#### Prepare Documents
| Document | Source |
|----------|--------|
| Wholesale invoice (3+ preferred) | WebstaurantStore, KaTom, etc. |
| Invoice must show: | Your LLC name, address, phone |
| Invoice must include: | Item descriptions, quantities, wholesale pricing |

#### Submit Ungating Request

**Path:** Seller Central → Inventory → Add a Product → Category Approval

**Select category:** Home & Kitchen → Kitchen → Kitchen Tools & Gadgets

**Upload:**
- Wholesale invoices (PDF)
- Resale certificate
- Brief letter explaining your business

**Sample letter:**
```
To Amazon Seller Performance,

I am requesting approval to sell in the Kitchen category. I operate [LLC Name], a licensed food truck and catering business in Jacksonville, Florida. 

I have attached:
- Wholesale invoices from authorized distributor [Name]
- Florida resale certificate
- Business license

My business has been operating since [Year] and I maintain a commercial kitchen operation. I am familiar with Amazon's selling policies and will ensure all products meet safety and quality standards.

Thank you for your consideration.

[Name]
[Title]
[LLC Name]
[Phone]
[Email]
```

#### Wait for Response
- Typical timeline: 2–6 weeks
- Check Seller Central daily
- Respond promptly to any requests for additional info

## Risk Mitigation

### What Could Go Wrong
| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| No eBay sales | Lower prices, improve photos, offer promotions |
| Wholesale rejection | Try more distributors, build eBay history first |
| Amazon ungating denied | Keep selling on eBay, retry in 3 months |
| Inventory doesn't sell | Bundle, discount, or liquidate |
| Cash flow tight | Start small, reinvest profits, avoid overbuying |
| Competition drops prices | Focus on lots/bundles, better service, faster shipping |
