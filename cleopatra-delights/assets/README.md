## Tone guidelines for replacement images

- **Colour palette**: warm amber / honey tones, deep brown backgrounds, matte not glossy
- **Lighting**: moody side-lighting, soft shadows, no harsh flash
- **Style**: flat-lay or 45° editorial angle; no white studio backgrounds
- **Consistency**: every image should share the same colour temperature and mood
